## About

* Prism is a shaderpack dedicated to **retro-stylized visuals and high-performance dithering techniques.** It aims to transform Minecraft's lighting into a low-fidelity style of lighting, featuring distinct ordered triangular dithering on surfaces and shadows.

## Main features

* **Visual Style**
    * **Ordered dithering:** Unique pixel-patterned gradients for lighting and shadows.
    * **Mellow visuals:** Maintains a calm, dim, pixel-grid-aligned look without heavy blurring.
    * **Clean atmosphere:** Enhanced skybox clarity and naturalistic birch forest lighting.

* **Mod compatibility**
    * Iris Shaders (Most stable loader)
    * OptiFine (Custom skyboxes and colored block lighting are **not supported**)
